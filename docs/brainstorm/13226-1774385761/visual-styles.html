CHIBI FANTASY

A — Chibi Fantasy

Rounded, cute creatures with big eyes and bold colors. Think Pokémon meets Studio Ghibli. Warm palette, magical feel. Best for kids 6–14, families.

✅ All ages ✅ Easy to generate ✅ High outdoor visibility ⚠️ Less unique

ADRIATIC MYTHS

B — Adriatic Myths

Creatures inspired by Italian/Adriatic mythology — sea spirits, ancient Roman beasts, Marche folklore. Deep blues, gold, terracotta. Unique to Pesaro, culturally rich.

✅ Unique identity ✅ Local pride ✅ Escape room feel ⚠️ Harder to generate

NEON WILD

C — Neon Wild

Dark background with glowing neon creature outlines. Cyberpunk meets nature. Teens love it. Works great on OLED screens. Best for teens 13–20, looks premium.

✅ Teen appeal ✅ Very distinctive ⚠️ Outdoor visibility ⚠️ Less family-friendly

STORYBOOK ADVENTURE

D — Storybook Adventure

Hand-illustrated look, warm earthy tones, like a children's book or Zelda: Breath of the Wild. Timeless, premium, works for all ages. Best quality/age range balance.

✅ All ages ✅ Premium feel ✅ Escape room fits ✅ Outdoor visibility
