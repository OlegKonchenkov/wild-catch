💡 Bottom Line
The entire MVP image set costs under $5 with either provider. Both have ~50% batch discounts for non-realtime generation. Recommendation: OpenAI gpt-image-1.5 — slightly cheaper per image, better consistency across a creature set, and excellent instruction-following for game-style artwork. Use Google AI Studio (free) to prototype styles before committing to paid generation.

Free prototyping: Google AI Studio gives you ~500–1000 free images/day. Use it to test art styles, prompts and creature designs first — then run the final paid batch with OpenAI or Imagen 4.
